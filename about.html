Caladiums are known for their big, heart-shaped leaves that display amazing color combiinations of white,pink,red and green. These are plants that thrive in hot, humid weather and that usually grow best in full to partial shade. Though caladiums rarely flower, their beatiful foliage guarantees a colorful show wherever they are planted -- beneath tress, between shrubs, in perrenial borders and in container. Caladium Plant is called as "Gabi-Gabi" in Visayan. In Philippines "Gabi-Gabi" began booming as Covid-19 virus arises, It serve as the entertainment so they wont bother thinking the Pandemic and also it can ease the boredom that are striking because of always staying at home as Government implenting that if possible, no one s allowed to go outside..
